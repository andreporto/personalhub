## 🧠 Transforming Leadership Through Artificial Intelligence

Welcome to the cutting-edge intersection of technology and leadership education. This program is designed for experienced software developers who are ready to expand their expertise into the realm of AI-driven decision making and strategic leadership.

---

## 🎯 Program Overview

### **MBA in AI Leadership**
- **Duration**: 2024-2025 Academic Year
- **Target Audience**: Senior Software Developers & Tech Leaders
- **Focus**: Bridging technical excellence with intelligent leadership

### **Core Learning Pillars**
1. **Intelligent Systems Architecture** - Designing AI-first solutions
2. **Data-Driven Decision Making** - Leveraging machine learning for strategy
3. **Leadership in the Age of AI** - Managing teams in an intelligent ecosystem
4. **Ethical AI Governance** - Building responsible AI systems
